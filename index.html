Press P to Pause/Resume the Minion and the Hero
Minion: Press WASD to queue directional movement while paused (This is now a Key Pressed Command) + Press 1,2,3,4 (Key Clicked) to queue locations to go to
Hero: Using the mouse, press left click to queue locations to go to, press middle mouse to queue location to attack at
Notice how the minion on the bottom still moves even though its paused, its not an RTP Game Object like the others
